About me

Front-end developer from Montpellier, France

I am a 28 year old woman from France who was lost for many, many years trying to figure out what I wanted to do in life. I would see everyone my age going off to University, knowing what they wanted to do while I went to University studying subjects I wasn't interested in. I felt I had to try something rather than doing nothing. Unfortunately, it never resulted to anything so I ended quiting so I could start working as a waitress to earn a living. When COVID hit, the hotel where I was employed at went out of business (because they decided it was a good time to renovate the hotel, which lead to bankrupcy), I was laid off and therefore was on the dole. There was a service included at the center of unemployement that offered professional help to find the right career path for individuals. I was quite reluctant at first as I had already twice before in my life seeked help by professionals for guidance, but it was more of a time waster than anything else. But after countless hours and months with this partucular professional, she finally helped me to find a path that suited me. To become a front-end developer!
